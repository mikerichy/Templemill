Templemill Livestream Service

Make your Livestreams engaging and professional.

In 2021, Livestreams are a great way to connect with your online community and invite guests from all over the world to your event. Our team makes sure that you're presented in the best way by streaming in 4k from our cinema grade cameras and professional lenses. Not only do we give you a crystal clear image but crisp audio from wireless and hand held microphones.



Our stream switcher will be monitoring the Livesteam while witching camera angles to keep the viewers engaged. The stream switcher is also cueing pre-prepared title cards and graphics for your stream such as slide shows and videos. Stability is very important to us so our cameras are hardwired to the stream switchers computer. The computer is connecting via ethernet directly to the router for high speed and secure internet connection.
